Project Overview

What is Treasure Map?

A personalized study abroad planning platform designed specifically for XJTLU students. We help you explore global universities, track application progress, and achieve your dreams.

Core Features

What can you do here?

Map Exploration - Interactive world map with university markers
Progress Tracking - Visual to-do lists and progress bars
Emotional Support - Mood tracking and encouragement
AI Assistant - Personalized recommendations and Q&A
Achievement System - Track your milestones

Target Users

Who is this for?

Year 1-2 Students - Early exploration and planning
Year 3-4 Students - Active application management
Advisors - Career and study planning support

Our system addresses common challenges: information scattered, complex procedures, high pressure, and lack of personalized guidance.
